# `<profile-card>`

### Example

```html
<profile-card name="Reggie"  position="KCLSU legend" image=""> </profile-card>
```

### Build your own

        position="${ifDefined(args.position || undefined)}"
        link="${ifDefined(args.link || undefined)}"
        secondlink="${ifDefined(args.secondlink || undefined)}"
        cta="${ifDefined(args.cta || undefined)}"
        secondcta="${ifDefined(args.secondcta || undefined)}"
        image="${ifDefined(args.image || undefined)}"
      > </profile-card>
    `}
  </Story>
</Canvas>
